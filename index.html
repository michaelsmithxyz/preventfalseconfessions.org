Support Massachusetts Bill H.1540

We are 8th graders at the Richard J. Murphy school. Our 8th grade class is working with Generation Citizen and we are working to address police brutality. We are making a petition to support getting audiovisual recordings and cameras in interrogation rooms. We're supporting a bill in Massachusetts called H1540. Please support us as we try to stop police brutality in our neighborhood, and around the world. We are hoping that it will make an impact to stop police brutality that is happening everywhere.

To support us, press on the link leading you to Change.org and sign our petition. The more the petition gets signed, the higher the chance we get to stop forced confessions. Another thing you can do is to watch our video and get a better understanding of what bill H1540 is! The last thing you can do is to call your representative and encourage them to support this bill!
